Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a beloved Italian pasta dish made with eggs, Parmesan cheese, pancetta (or bacon), and black pepper. It‚Äôs creamy, rich, and comforting ‚Äî yet incredibly simple to make in under 30 minutes. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or an elegant meal!

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese (plus extra for serving)
  • 1 clove garlic (optional, lightly crushed)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

For Garnish:

  • Chopped parsley (optional)
  • Extra Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente. Reserve ¬Ω cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Cook the Pancetta: In a pan, cook pancetta (or bacon) over medium heat until crispy. Add garlic if using, then remove it after 1 minute.
  3. Make the Sauce: In a bowl, whisk eggs, Parmesan, and a pinch of black pepper until well combined.
  4. Combine Everything: Add the hot pasta to the pan with pancetta. Remove from heat, then pour in the egg mixture, tossing quickly to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water gradually if it‚Äôs too thick.
  5. Serve: Plate and top with extra cheese, parsley, and freshly cracked black pepper. Serve immediately.

Notes

  • For a richer flavor, use Pecorino Romano cheese instead of Parmesan.
  • Never add cream ‚Äî authentic Carbonara gets its creaminess from eggs and cheese.
  • Use freshly ground black pepper for the best aroma.
