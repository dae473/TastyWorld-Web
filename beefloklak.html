Description

Lok Lak is a traditional Cambodian dish made of tender beef cubes stir-fried with a savory, slightly sweet sauce. It‚Äôs usually served with white rice, fresh vegetables, and a fried egg on top ‚Äî a simple yet flavorful meal loved across Cambodia.

Ingredients

For the Beef Marinade:

  • 300g beef (sirloin or tenderloin, thinly sliced)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

For the Sauce:

  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

For Serving:

  • Steamed jasmine rice
  • 1 fried egg per serving
  • Slices of tomato, cucumber, and lettuce

Directions

  1. Marinate the Beef: Combine sliced beef with sauces, sugar, pepper, and garlic. Let rest for 15‚Äì20 minutes.
  2. Cook the Beef: Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry beef until browned and cooked (about 3‚Äì5 minutes).
  3. Prepare the Sauce: Mix lime juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  4. Fry the Egg: In another pan, fry the egg sunny-side up.
  5. Assemble the Plate: Serve beef with rice, fried egg, and fresh vegetables. Add dipping sauce on the side.

Notes

  • Add ketchup or soy sauce for extra flavor.
  • Can substitute beef with chicken or pork.
  • Enjoy with iced tea or lime soda for a refreshing meal!
